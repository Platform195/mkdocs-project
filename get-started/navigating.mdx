---
title: Navigating Uplift Hub
description: A tour of the app shell — sidebar, top bar, and primary screens.
---

The new app is organised around **Accounts → Campaigns → Line Items → Products**,
with a persistent sidebar on the left and a slim top bar for context, search,
notifications and your profile.

<Frame caption="Uplift Hub app shell">
  <img src="/images/app-shell.png" alt="Uplift Hub app shell" />
</Frame>

1. **Account selector** — switch between advertiser/account workspaces.
2. **Primary navigation** — Dashboard, Accounts, Campaigns, Line Items, Products,
   Resources, Reporting and Configuration.
3. **Top bar** — page context, breadcrumbs, sidebar toggle and global actions.
4. **Search, notifications and profile** — real-time search, unread updates,
   and your account.

<Tip>Collapse the sidebar at any time using the toggle in the top bar.</Tip>
