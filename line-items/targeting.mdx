Keyword targeting lets you serve ads to users based on specific terms — such as destinations, interests, or behaviours — so your ads reach the most relevant audience. Once your keyword list is enabled, you can apply targeting directly on a line item and forecast the available inventory in real time.

---

## Getting keyword targeting enabled

Keyword targeting is not active by default. To enable it, contact your account manager and provide a keyword file as a CSV.

The CSV must follow this structure:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Name** | A human-readable label for the keyword group (e.g. `Destination`, `Destination DE`) |
| **Key** | The targeting parameter key used by the ad server (e.g. `dest`) |
| **Value** | The specific value to match against (e.g. `dubai`, `phuket`) |

**Example:**

```csv
Name,Key,Value
Destination,dest,dubai
Destination,dest,abu dhabi
Destination,dest,ras al khaimah
Destination,dest,fujairah
Destination,dest,sharjah
Destination,dest,maldives
Destination,dest,sri lanka
Destination,dest,seychelles
Destination,dest,bangkok
Destination,dest,phuket
Destination,dest,koh samui
Destination,dest,mauritius
Destination,dest,bali
Destination DE,dest,dubai
Destination DE,dest,abu dhabi
```

<Tip>
  You can define multiple keyword groups in a single file. Use the **Name** column to group related values together — for example, separate groups for different markets or languages.
</Tip>

Once your account manager has processed the file, your keyword groups will appear in the targeting panel on your line items.

<Note>
  Keyword targeting is enabled across your entire client account, but it only applies to specific products. If you don't see the targeting panel on a line item, the product associated with that line item may not support keyword targeting. Contact your account manager to confirm which products have it enabled.
</Note>

## Applying targeting to a line item

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the line item">
    Navigate to the line item you want to target — either from the sidebar **Line Items** page or from the parent campaign.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the targeting panel">
    On the line item **Overview** tab, scroll down to the **Targeting** section. This panel is only visible on line items delivered through Uplift Ads with keyword targeting enabled on your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your keyword groups">
    Choose one or more keyword groups from the list. Each group represents a set of values your account manager loaded from your keyword file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the forecast">
    The forecast updates in real time as you select targeting and adjust the line item's date range. Use this to confirm there is sufficient available inventory before saving.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click <Ui>Save Changes</Ui> in the top right to apply the targeting.
  </Step>
</Steps>
