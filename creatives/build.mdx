Creatives are added at the line item level, then rolled up into a read-only
view at the campaign level. The build process differs slightly depending on
whether the product uses a **native ad** format or a **fixed-size ad** format.

---

## Step 1 — Open the Creatives tab

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the line item">
    Navigate to the line item from the sidebar <Ui>Line Items</Ui> page or
    through its parent campaign.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to Creatives">
    Select the <Ui>Creatives</Ui> tab, then click <Ui>Add creative</Ui>.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Step 2 — Add your assets

How you fill in the creative depends on the product type.

### Native ads

For native products, enter your assets directly into the **Creative Template**
fields:

| Field | What to provide |
|---|---|
| Headline | Short ad headline |
| Body copy | Supporting text |
| Logo | Brand logo file |
| Image | Hero image at the required dimensions |
| Click-through URL | Destination page |
| Tracking script | Third-party impression/click tracker (if applicable) |

### Fixed-size ads

For fixed-size display products, upload your assets under the **Media Assets**
section:

| Field | What to provide |
|---|---|
| Ad files | Completed ad at the correct dimensions (check product spec) |
| Click-through URL | Destination page |
| Tracking script | Third-party tracker (if applicable) |
| Third-party ad tag | Fully hosted tag, if the client is supplying the ad server |

<Note>
  **File naming** — use clear, descriptive filenames. If you're capturing
  screenshots or video evidence during the campaign, follow the convention
  `UH_[LineItemRef]_[SequenceNumber]` (e.g. `UH_US006_001`).
</Note>

---

## Step 3 — Preview

Once assets are uploaded, use the preview to check the creative renders
correctly before going live. If a native ad, the preview shows how the ad
will appear in situ.

---

## Viewing all creatives on a campaign

To see every creative across all line items in one place, open the campaign
and switch to its <Ui>Creatives</Ui> tab. This is a read-only roll-up — edits
are made at the line item level.

---

## Who uploads what

<Tip>
  If the **client** has supplied finished creatives, upload them directly.
  If **ad design is still required**, the designer uploads the files to the
  Creatives tab for client approval before go-live.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Creatives must be approved before a line item can be set to
  <Ui>Ready Live</Ui> or <Ui>Live</Ui>. If assets are missing or unapproved,
  the line item will not deliver.
</Warning>
