# Adding a new account

An **Account** is the buying company of a campaign — usually the advertiser.
It's the top of the Uplift Hub hierarchy; every campaign and line item belongs
to one.

## Steps

1. From the left sidebar, select <span class="ui-ref">Accounts</span>.
2. Click <span class="ui-ref">Add new account</span> in the top right.
3. Enter the account details. Required fields are marked with a red
   asterisk **&ast;**. Click <span class="ui-ref">Create account</span> to save.

!!! tip "Tip — Internal IDs"
    Include account IDs from your internal systems in the account name for easy
    reference. You can also add multiple IDs in the
    <span class="ui-ref">Product IDs</span> field.
