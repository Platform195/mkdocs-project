# Tracking performance

Once a campaign is live you can track its progress in three places: the
<span class="ui-ref">Reporting</span> tab on a line item, the
<span class="ui-ref">Reporting</span> tab on a campaign, or globally from the
sidebar.

## Automated reporting

Line items delivered via **Uplift Ads** or **Uplift Ads+** have automated
reporting. Each row shows campaign name, dates, goal volume, impressions,
clicks, CTR, pacing and completion.

!!! tip "Pacing vs. completion"
    Pacing higher than completion = front-loaded delivery. Pacing lower than
    completion = at risk of under-delivering.

## Manual reporting

Line items without automated reporting need someone to enter metrics manually
in the line item's <span class="ui-ref">Reporting</span> tab. Reporting fields
can be customised by product or by client — email
[support@uplifthub.io](mailto:support@uplifthub.io) for changes or new fields.
