# Signing in

Open Uplift Hub in your browser at [app.uplifthub.io](https://app.uplifthub.io),
enter your email and password, and select <span class="ui-ref">Login</span>.

If you've forgotten your password, use <span class="ui-ref">Forgot password?</span>
on the sign-in screen and follow the link sent to your inbox.

!!! tip "Single sign-on"
    If your team uses SSO, ask your Platform 195 representative to enable it
    for your domain.
