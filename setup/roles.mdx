---

Uplift Hub includes three roles designed for client users. Each role is scoped to give the right level of access for different responsibilities within your organisation.

---

## Role summary

| Role | Best for | Key distinction |
|---|---|---|
| **ClientCampaignManager** | Client-side campaign leads | Full read/write access across campaigns, line items, and creatives ‚Äî including adding campaigns and editing settings |
| **ClientOperativeRole** | Day-to-day client operators | Broad read access and the ability to submit comments and upload creatives, but no destructive or structural actions |
| **SalesManager** | Client sales and account contacts | Dashboard and reporting visibility with limited write access ‚Äî focused on oversight rather than operation |

---

## ClientCampaignManager

This role is designed for clients who take an active management role in their campaigns. They can create and configure campaigns, manage line items end-to-end, upload creatives, and access reporting ‚Äî but they cannot access internal comments or administrative configuration.

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Dashboard">
    - `hub.dashboard` ‚Äî access the main dashboard
    - `hub.dashboard.accounts` ‚Äî view accounts dashboard
    - `hub.dashboard.upcoming` ‚Äî view upcoming campaigns
    - `hub.dashboard.live` ‚Äî view live campaigns
    - `hub.dashboard.completed` ‚Äî view completed campaigns
    - `hub.dashboard.paused` ‚Äî view paused campaigns
    - `hub.dashboard.notifications` ‚Äî view notifications
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Accounts">
    - `hub.account` ‚Äî access accounts
    - `hub.account.export` ‚Äî export account data
    - `hub.account.overview` ‚Äî view account overview
    - `hub.account.settings` ‚Äî view and edit account settings
    - `hub.account.settings.delete` ‚Äî delete account settings
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Campaigns">
    - `hub.campaign` ‚Äî access campaigns
    - `hub.campaign.add` ‚Äî create new campaigns
    - `hub.campaign.export` ‚Äî export campaign data
    - `hub.campaign.overview` ‚Äî view campaign overview
    - `hub.campaign.brief` ‚Äî view campaign brief
    - `hub.campaign.creatives` ‚Äî view campaign creatives
    - `hub.campaign.evidence` ‚Äî view campaign evidence
    - `hub.campaign.reporting` ‚Äî access campaign reporting
    - `hub.campaign.comments` ‚Äî view comments
    - `hub.campaign.comments.all` ‚Äî view all comments
    - `hub.campaign.comments.all.add` ‚Äî add comments
    - `hub.campaign.documents` ‚Äî view documents
    - `hub.campaign.settings` ‚Äî view campaign settings
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Line Items">
    - `hub.lineitem` ‚Äî access line items
    - `hub.lineitem.export` ‚Äî export line item data
    - `hub.lineitem.overview` ‚Äî view line item overview
    - `hub.lineitem.brief` ‚Äî view line item brief
    - `hub.lineitem.creatives` ‚Äî view creatives
    - `hub.lineitem.creatives.export` ‚Äî export creatives
    - `hub.lineitem.creatives.creative` ‚Äî view individual creatives
    - `hub.lineitem.creatives.comments` ‚Äî view creative comments
    - `hub.lineitem.creatives.comments.all` ‚Äî view all creative comments
    - `hub.lineitem.creatives.comments.all.add` ‚Äî add creative comments
    - `hub.lineitem.evidence` ‚Äî view evidence
    - `hub.lineitem.reporting` ‚Äî access line item reporting
    - `hub.lineitem.comments` ‚Äî view comments
    - `hub.lineitem.comments.all` ‚Äî view all comments
    - `hub.lineitem.comments.all.add` ‚Äî add comments
    - `hub.lineitem.documents` ‚Äî view documents
    - `hub.lineitem.settings` ‚Äî view line item settings
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Products & Resources">
    - `hub.product` ‚Äî access products
    - `hub.product.export` ‚Äî export product data
    - `hub.product.settings` ‚Äî view product settings
    - `hub.resources` ‚Äî access resources
    - `hub.reporting` ‚Äî access reporting
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  ClientCampaignManager cannot access internal comments (`hub.campaign.comments.internal`) or any administrative configuration.
</Note>
