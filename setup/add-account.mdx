import { Ui } from "/snippets/ui.mdx";

An **Account** is the buying company of a campaign — usually the advertiser.
It's the top of the Uplift Hub hierarchy; every campaign and line item belongs
to one.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Accounts">
    From the left sidebar, select <Ui>Accounts</Ui>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add new account">
    Click <Ui>Add new account</Ui> in the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in details">
    Enter the account details. Required fields are marked with a red
    asterisk **&ast;**. Click <Ui>Create account</Ui> to save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Internal IDs** — include account IDs from your internal systems in the
  account name for easy reference. You can also add multiple IDs in the
  <Ui>Product IDs</Ui> field.
</Tip>
