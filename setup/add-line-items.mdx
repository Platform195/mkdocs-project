If you selected a **Package** type campaign, line items are generated
automatically. If you selected **Custom**, you'll add line items individually
to match the media plan.

## Two ways to add a line item

<Steps>
  <Step title="From inside a campaign">
    Open a campaign, switch to the <Ui>Line items</Ui> tab and click
    <Ui>Add new line item</Ui>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="From the Line Items page">
    Select <Ui>Line Items</Ui> in the sidebar, click <Ui>Add new line item</Ui>,
    and assign it to a campaign from the dropdown.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add details

Provide as much information as possible. Use <Ui>Notes</Ui> for special
instructions (targeting, prioritisation) and include links to the media plan or
brief.

<Tip>
  **Need help choosing a product?** Leave a comment on the campaign's
  <Ui>Comments</Ui> tab. Type **@** followed by a teammate or your Platform 195
  representative to notify them.
</Tip>
