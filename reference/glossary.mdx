Quick reference for the language used throughout Uplift Hub.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Account">
    The buying company on a campaign — usually the advertiser. Sits at the top
    of the Uplift Hub hierarchy.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ad creative">
    A tab at the campaign and line item levels. Finalised ad creatives are
    uploaded here.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Audit">
    A tab at the campaign and line item levels showing a chronological log of
    every change.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Campaign">
    The collection of activity sold under a single contract, purchase or
    insertion order.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Evidence">
    Storage for screenshots of ads in situ — proof a campaign is live or
    finished.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Line item">
    The individual rows that make up a campaign — each one mirroring a line on
    the media plan and exactly one ad product.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Media assets">
    The individual components that make up a finished ad: images, logos, copy,
    click-through URLs, tracking scripts and third-party hosted ad tags.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pacing">
    Percentage shown next to live line items in Reporting indicating how
    on-track delivery is.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Packages">
    A predefined grouping of products sold together. Packages can be **fixed**
    (each product's price is locked) or **blended** (each product's value is a
    percentage of the campaign budget).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Product">
    The individual ad product sold to the advertiser. Each line item is
    associated with exactly one product.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Product ID">
    Optional field on an account used to record IDs from your internal systems.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Uplift Ads">
    The ad-server engine that delivers on-site ads for Platform 195 clients.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Uplift Ads+">
    Platform 195's audience-extension offering — activates ads across networks
    including search and social.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
